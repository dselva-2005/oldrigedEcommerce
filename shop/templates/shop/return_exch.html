Return & Exchange Policy

We want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. Please read our return and exchange policy below:

1. Return Eligibility
  • Items can be returned within **7 days** of delivery.
  • Products must be unused, in their original packaging, and with all tags intact.
  • Returns are not accepted for perishable items, personalized products, or hygiene-sensitive goods.
2. Exchange Policy
  • Exchanges are allowed for defective or incorrect products.
  • Customers must raise an exchange request within **7 days** of receiving the order.
3. Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, refunds will be processed within **5-7 business days** to your original payment method.

4. Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect.

5. How to Initiate a Return/Exchange

To start a return or exchange, please contact our customer support at Contact Us.

For more details, please reach out to our support team.
